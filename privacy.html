Your Privacy & Platform Usage

LearnPro is committed to transparency, user protection, and a secure learning experience. Below are our Privacy Policy and Terms of Use explaining how our platform works and how learners should responsibly use the application.

Privacy Policy

LearnPro respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, store, and use your information while using our learning platform, webinars, courses, community, and support services.

Information We Collect

  • Basic account information such as your name, email address, and profile details.
  • Learning activity including enrolled courses, progress tracking, webinar participation, and certificates.
  • Communication records when contacting support or participating in discussions.
  • Technical information such as browser type, device information, and session analytics.

How We Use Your Information

  • To personalize your learning experience and course recommendations.
  • To improve platform performance and user experience.
  • To provide technical support and account assistance.
  • To notify users about new courses, webinars, updates, and security alerts.

Security & Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect user data and maintain platform integrity. While no system is completely immune to security risks, LearnPro continuously improves infrastructure and monitoring systems to reduce vulnerabilities.

LearnPro does not sell personal user data to third parties. User information is only used to improve platform services and educational experiences.

Terms of Use

By accessing or using LearnPro, you agree to comply with the following terms and conditions. These guidelines help maintain a safe, respectful, and productive learning environment for all users.

How To Use The Application

  • Create an account using accurate and verifiable information.
  • Browse and enroll in courses based on your interests or career goals.
  • Track learning progress through dashboards and progress indicators.
  • Join webinars and live sessions using your registered account.
  • Participate respectfully within discussions, comments, and community spaces.
  • Access certificates and learning resources after course completion.

User Responsibilities

  • Users are responsible for maintaining account confidentiality and password security.
  • Course materials must not be redistributed, copied, or resold without permission.
  • Users must not upload harmful, offensive, or illegal content.
  • Abusive behavior, harassment, impersonation, or fraudulent activity may result in account suspension.

Community Conduct

LearnPro encourages respectful collaboration and healthy interaction within the learning community. Users should communicate professionally and supportively while engaging with instructors and other learners.

Platform Availability

While LearnPro aims to provide uninterrupted access, temporary maintenance, updates, or technical issues may occasionally affect platform availability. We continuously work to improve system reliability and performance.

Continued use of LearnPro confirms your acceptance of these policies and terms.
